subroutine comp_sije ( real, dimension(lx1*ly1*lz1,ldim,ldim)  gije)

Compute symmetric part of a tensor gije for element. Does not call any other subroutine, but simply evaluates:

\[ G_{ij} = \frac{1}{2}(G_{ij} + G_{ji}) \]

\[ G_{ji} = G_{ij} \]

.
